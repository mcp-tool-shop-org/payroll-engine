# payroll-engine

## What This Does (Prototype)

⚠️ **Prototype Status** — Payroll processing and management system.

Processes payroll, manages employee compensation, tax calculations,
and generates payroll reports and documentation.

## Features

- Payroll calculation
- Tax computation
- Deduction management
- Employee records
- Report generation
- Compliance tracking

## Architecture

- Calculation engine
- Tax rule management
- Employee database
- Reporting system

## Key Notes

- Prototype payroll system
- Not for production use without review
- Tax rules are jurisdiction-specific
- Subject to regulation changes
